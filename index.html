Our Trading Story

Angalaeswari Trading Company was established in 1996. a mission to bring the finest agricultural products from Vilathikulam to markets across India.

Located in the Vember road near taluk office of Vilathikulam (Pincode: 628907), we specialize in sourcing premium quality Chilli,Millet,Pulses,Cotton,Oilseeds, Corn & Coriander directly from local farmers who follow traditional farming methods.

We maintain strict quality control at every stage - from farm selection to packaging and delivery. Our products are known for their authentic taste, vibrant color, and superior quality that meets both domestic and international standards.
